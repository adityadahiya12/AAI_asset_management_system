Operations Asset Categories

Detailed classification of all operational assets managed by AAI's Operations department

Ground Support & Airside Operations Equipment

Category 1
Asset Type Description
Runway Lighting System Edge lights, threshold lights, stop bar lights
Taxiway Guidance Signs Illuminated signs with position & direction
Airfield Ground Lighting (AGL) Control Units Central control for lighting sequences
Stop Bars Controlled red lights at runway/taxiway intersections
Windsocks / Wind Cones For pilot visibility of wind direction
Visual Docking Guidance Systems (VDGS) Directs aircraft to park at gates
Runway Surface Condition Sensors Detects friction, contamination (especially in monsoon/snow)

Terminal & Passenger Services Assets

Category 2
Asset Type Description
Passenger Information Display Systems (PIDS) Displays flight info inside terminal
Queue Management Systems Token machines, digital boards
Public Address (PA) System For announcements inside terminal
Signages & Wayfinding Boards Static and digital signboards across terminal
Baggage Handling System (BHS) Conveyors, sorters, barcode readers
Elevators/Escalators Installed inside terminals; includes AMC contracts
Trolleys & Trolley Management Units Passenger-use carts and tracking systems

Safety & Emergency Response Equipment

Category 3
Asset Type Description
Fire Extinguishers ABC/DCP/CO2 types with refill date tracking
Fire Hydrant Systems Pumps, pipes, hoses â€“ fixed installations
Smoke Detectors & Alarms Installed in terminals, control rooms
Emergency Evacuation Equipment Wheelchairs, stretchers, first aid kits
Incident Recording Systems Body cams, emergency phones, black-box-style logs
CCTV Surveillance Systems Cameras placed for passenger monitoring (linked to security also)

Vehicle & Mobility Assets

Category 4
Asset Type Description
Follow-Me Vehicles Used to guide aircraft on the ground
Ambulances For passenger/staff emergencies
Apron Buses Transport passengers between aircraft and terminal
Electric Buggies / Carts Used for passenger movement (seniors, disabled)
Operations SUVs Used by duty officers for airside inspection
Two-Way Radios / Handheld Devices Communication tools for ground staff

Control & Reporting Systems

Category 5
Asset Type Description
Airport Operations Database (AODB) Central system for operations and schedule tracking
Flight Information Display System (FIDS) Syncs with ATC & airlines
Incident Management Systems For safety, complaints, emergency reporting
Daily Operations Logbooks Digital or physical logs of shift activities
Terminal Occupancy Systems Real-time crowd density data
Environmental Monitoring Systems For temperature, air quality, noise monitoring

Office & Utility Assets

Category 6
Asset Type Description
Operations Desktops & Laptops Used by Ops officers for reports/logs
Printers/Scanners Installed in operation rooms and lounges
PA Microphones/Stations Used for terminal announcements
Air Conditioning Units Especially in control rooms & lounges
Stationery & Registers Shift logs, duty rosters, report books

Operations Asset Management

Comprehensive tracking and management of all operational assets across AAI facilities.

Our system provides complete visibility into the lifecycle of every operational asset, from procurement to retirement. With detailed categorization, tracking, and reporting capabilities, we ensure optimal utilization of resources while maintaining safety and compliance standards.

  • Real-time asset tracking and status monitoring
  • Automated maintenance and inspection alerts
  • Detailed reporting and analytics
  • Integration with operations ticketing system
  • Safety compliance tracking
