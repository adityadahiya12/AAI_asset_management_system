Asset Lifecycle Workflow

From onboarding to offboarding - seamless asset management for all employees

Comprehensive Asset Lifecycle

Our end-to-end process ensures efficient management of all employee assets

1

Request Initiation

The asset lifecycle begins with a request from either HR (for new employees) or department heads (for existing employees).

  • Automated request forms based on employee role
  • Pre-approved asset bundles for standard positions
  • Custom request options for specialized roles
  • Multi-level approval workflow
Approval Required HR Initiated
2

Approval Process

Requests are routed through the appropriate approval channels based on asset type and cost.

  • Department head approval for standard assets
  • Finance approval for high-value items
  • IT security review for technology assets
  • Real-time status tracking
Multi-level Audit Trail
3

Asset Procurement

Once approved, assets are either allocated from inventory or purchased from vendors.

  • Automated inventory checks
  • Integration with approved vendors
  • Purchase order generation
  • Delivery tracking
Inventory Check Vendor Integration
4

Asset Preparation

Assets are configured and prepared for employee use according to their role requirements.

  • Standard software loadouts
  • Security configurations
  • Access permissions setup
  • Quality assurance testing
IT Configuration QA Testing
5

Employee Onboarding

Assets are assigned to the employee during their onboarding process.

  • Digital acceptance forms
  • Training materials delivery
  • Asset condition documentation
  • Warranty information
Digital Signature Training
6

Daily Management

Ongoing asset management during the employee lifecycle.

  • Maintenance requests
  • Upgrades and replacements
  • Temporary allocations
  • Access permission changes
Maintenance Upgrades
7

Offboarding Process

When an employee leaves, assets are retrieved and processed.

  • Automated retrieval checklist
  • Condition assessment
  • Data sanitization
  • Reallocation preparation
Retrieval Data Security
8

Asset Retirement

End-of-life processing for retired assets.

  • Secure disposal procedures
  • Data destruction certification
  • Recycling documentation
  • Inventory updates
Disposal Recycling
