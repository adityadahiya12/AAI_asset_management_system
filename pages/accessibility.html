Accessibility Statement

Commitment to Accessibility

The Airport Authority of India (AAI) is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to the Asset Management System.

Conformance Status

The AAI Asset Management System partially conforms to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. Partial conformance means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Level AA We aim to meet all WCAG 2.1 Level AA requirements by May 2025.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features in our system:

Keyboard Navigation

The system can be operated using only a keyboard, without requiring specific timings for individual keystrokes. All functionality is available through keyboard interfaces.

Text Alternatives

All non-text content has text alternatives that serve equivalent purposes, except for decorative elements.

Color & Contrast

The visual presentation of text and images has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1, except for large text which has a contrast ratio of at least 3:1.

Responsive Design

Content can be presented without loss of information or functionality, and without requiring scrolling in two dimensions for typical screen sizes.

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of the AAI Asset Management System, there may be some limitations:

  • Some complex data tables may not be fully accessible to screen readers
  • Older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Some third-party integrations may have limited accessibility
  • Certain dynamic content updates may not be announced by screen readers

We are actively working to address these limitations in future updates.

These technologies are used in conformance with the accessibility standards mentioned above.

Assessment Approach

AAI assessed the accessibility of the Asset Management System through the following approaches:

  • Self-evaluation using automated tools and manual testing
  • External accessibility audit conducted in May 2025
  • User testing with employees with disabilities

Compatibility

The AAI Asset Management System is designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Zoom text up to 200%
  • Major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)

Ongoing Efforts

Our accessibility efforts are ongoing as we continue to improve the system. Current initiatives include:

  • Monthly accessibility audits
  • Quarterly training for development teams
  • Accessibility testing with real users
  • Regular updates to meet evolving standards
