Common Login Issues

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your Employee ID and registered email
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email

After multiple failed login attempts, your account may be locked for security reasons.

Please contact the help desk at 1800-123-4567 to unlock your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

To register:

  1. Go to the registration page
  2. Fill out all required information
  3. Submit the form
  4. You'll receive your login credentials via email within 24 hours

If you're having trouble logging in:

  • Make sure you're using the correct Employee ID and password
  • Check that your caps lock is off
  • Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  • If problems persist, contact the help desk at 1800-123-4567

To report technical issues:

  1. Go to the Help Center
  2. Fill out the support form with details of your issue
  3. Submit the form
  4. You'll receive a ticket number for tracking
  5. Our team will contact you within 1 business day
