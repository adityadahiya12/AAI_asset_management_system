Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to the Airport Authority of India (AAI) Asset Management System. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our asset management system.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including:

Personal Information

  • Employee identification details (name, employee ID, department)
  • Contact information (email, phone number, office location)
  • Job title and role within AAI
  • Access credentials for system authentication

Asset Information

  • Details of assets assigned to you (IT equipment, access cards, tools)
  • Usage logs and maintenance records
  • Location tracking data for mobile assets

Technical Information

  • IP addresses and device information
  • System access logs and timestamps
  • Browser type and operating system

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our asset management services
  • To manage asset allocation and tracking
  • To process IT support tickets and maintenance requests
  • To improve system functionality and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and AAI policies
  • To ensure system security and prevent fraud

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Authorized AAI personnel within your department
  • IT support teams for troubleshooting purposes
  • Security personnel for access control verification
  • Government authorities when required by law

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access controls
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access

Your Rights

As an AAI employee, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data when no longer needed
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact the AAI Data Protection Officer.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain sessions
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze system performance and usage patterns

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect system functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the AAI internal communication channels.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact:
