Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

To request a new asset:

  1. Log in to the AAI Asset Management System
  2. Navigate to the "Asset Requests" section
  3. Complete the new asset request form
  4. Submit for approval by your department head

You'll receive notifications about the status of your request. Standard processing time is 3-5 business days.

If your access card stops functioning:

  1. Try using it at different access points to confirm the issue
  2. Check for visible damage to the card
  3. Contact the 24/7 helpdesk immediately at +91-11-2467-XXXX
  4. Visit your nearest security office for a temporary pass if needed

Replacement cards are typically issued within 24 hours.

Immediately report lost or stolen assets:

  1. Call the emergency support line: +91-11-2467-EMER (3637)
  2. File a police report if theft is suspected
  3. Submit a formal report through the asset management system
  4. Notify your department head

Delays in reporting may result in security violations.

To check your ticket status:

  1. Log in to the AAI Asset Management System
  2. Go to the "IT Tickets" section
  3. Locate your ticket in the "My Tickets" tab
  4. View current status and any technician notes

You'll also receive email updates when your ticket status changes.

The offboarding process includes:

  1. HR will initiate the asset return workflow
  2. You'll receive a checklist of all assigned assets
  3. Schedule a return appointment with IT/Admin
  4. All assets must be returned in working condition
  5. Complete the asset clearance form

Failure to return assets may delay your clearance process.
